Education Access

While the Philippines boasts a high literacy rate of 99.27% as of 2021, challenges persist in ensuring equitable access to quality education across different regions and socio-economic groups.

Social Media Influence

The Philippines is known as the "Social Media Capital of the World," with the average internet user spending almost 4 hours per day on social media platforms.

Youth Activism

60% of Filipino youth like or share political posts, 54% seek news on political issues, and 49% discuss such issues either in person or online.

Rizal's Legacy

Dr. José Rizal emphasized education as a vital tool for empowerment and national progress. Current challenges highlight the need to reinforce his advocacy for accessible education.

Kahalagahan ng Wika

Wika at Pambansang Identidad

Ang wika ay nagpapahayag ng ating kultura at tradisyon. Sa pamamagitan ng ating wika, naipapahayag ang ating mga saloobin, pananaw, at mga karanasan.

Rizal at ang Sariling Wika

Si Jose Rizal ay nagtaguyod ng paggamit ng sariling wika bilang paraan ng pagpapakita ng pagmamahal sa bayan at pagpapanatili ng ating kultura.

Ang hindi magmahal sa sariling wika ay higit pa sa hayop at malansang isda.
- Jose Rizal
